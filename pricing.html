What happens at the end of my 14-day trial?

Nothing — until you tell us. We don't ask for a card upfront and we don't auto-charge. On day 14 we send a single email summarizing what you built and offering a plan. Cancel anytime; your data exports to CSV in one click.

Will you migrate my data from HubSpot, Mailchimp, or Calendly?

Yes — on Growth and above we run a guided migration. We pull contacts, deals, automations, and campaigns and rebuild them inside Clientlink so your team comes back on Monday to a workspace that already works. Typical migration takes 3–5 business days.

How are seats and contacts metered?

A seat is anyone with login access to your workspace. A contact is a unique person with at least one identifier — an email or phone — that you've stored in CRM. Imports are unlimited; we only meter your active count.

Can I run multiple brands or clients in one account?

That's what sub-accounts are for. Growth includes 3 sub-accounts; Scale is unlimited. Each sub-account has its own CRM, sending domain, branding, and team — billed under a single parent invoice.

Do you offer a non-profit, education, or startup discount?

Yes. Registered non-profits and accredited education customers get 30% off any plan. Pre-seed startups (less than $1M raised) get Growth at $49 for 12 months — apply through the demo form and we'll set it up.

What about HIPAA / SOC 2 / GDPR?

SOC 2 Type II and GDPR are standard on every plan. HIPAA-eligible workloads run on Scale with a signed BAA — we restrict the PHI surface to the CRM and supported integrations. Security questionnaires available on request.

What if my volume changes mid-month?

You won't get hit with a surprise bill. We email you at 80% of any meter (sends, SMS, AI drafts), recommend a plan move, and only count overages from the moment you confirm. No throttling, no silent shutoffs.

Every meeting booked. Every no-show chased.

Round-robin, group, collective, and one-off meetings — branded, embedded, and synced to every calendar your team already uses. Reminders go out by SMS and email automatically.

  • Round-robin, collective, group, and one-off bookings
  • Auto-routing by territory, plan size, or AI score
  • Branded booking pages on your domain
  • SMS + email reminders, no-show recovery flows

Features

Scheduling that sells.

Round-robin routing

Distribute meetings evenly across your team. Set rules by territory, deal size, or availability.

Branded pages

Custom booking pages on your domain. Embed them in funnels, emails, or anywhere with a snippet.

Auto-reminders

SMS and email reminders at 24h, 1h, and 15 minutes. No-show recovery flow kicks in automatically.

Calendar sync

Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal. Buffer times, daily limits, and availability rules — all respected.

Group & collective

One link for the whole team. Collective scheduling finds a time everyone is free at once.

Intake forms

Collect context before the meeting. Answers flow straight into the CRM contact record.

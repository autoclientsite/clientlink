Features

Your reputation, on autopilot.

Automated requests

Send review requests by SMS or email right after the visit. Timing and message are set once and run forever.

AI reply drafts

Clientlink drafts a thoughtful reply in your brand voice for every review. Your team approves in one click.

Unified inbox

Google, Facebook, Yelp, Healthgrades, and more — all reviews in one place, sorted by urgency.

Negative alerts

Get notified instantly when a low-star review lands. Suggested resolution flow kicks in automatically.

Social listening

Monitor brand mentions across social platforms and respond before a small comment becomes a big problem.

Review analytics

Track your average rating, response rate, and review volume over time. Export for reporting.

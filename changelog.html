AI Pipeline Copilot goes GA.

Six months in beta, 2,800 customers, 4.1M deals processed. The AI Pipeline Copilot is now generally available on every Growth and Scale workspace.

The first sales assistant that knows your whole pipeline.

Pipeline Copilot reads every conversation, deal note, calendar event, and email reply in your workspace — then drafts the next move, flags slipping deals, and tells your reps which prospect to call before standup.

  • "Who's slipping" — daily morning brief, in your inbox by 7 AM
  • One-click "draft the next email" on any deal
  • Forecasting model retrained nightly on your data, never anyone else's
  • SOC 2 + GDPR · your data never leaves the platform

New Improvements Fixes

Funnel templates reach 140.

Twelve new conversion-tested funnel templates land — including SaaS trial activation, healthcare patient intake (HIPAA-eligible), and coaching enrollment flows.

New

  • 12 new funnel templates. Including saas-trial-activation, patient-intake, coaching-enrollment, local-services-quote.
  • Per-step A/B test history. See every variant you've shipped and which one won, scoped per page step.

  • Funnel editor: 38% faster initial load on large funnels (lazy-loaded preview frames).
  • SMS sender now auto-warms new short codes across days 1–14.
  • Booking widget: respect the visitor's locale for date format and first-day-of-week.

Fixes

  • Fixed: tags applied during bulk import sometimes lagged 30s on workspaces with >100K contacts.
  • Fixed: timezone drift on recurring meetings created during DST changeovers.
  • Fixed: SAML SSO redirect loop on workspaces using ADFS-issued metadata.

v4.16.2
ImprovementsFixes

Bulk import gets 10× faster.

We rewrote the contact import pipeline. A 250K-row CSV that used to take 28 minutes now finishes in 2 minutes 40 seconds, end to end — including deduplication, enrichment, and tag application.

Improvements

  • Contact import: 10× faster on large CSVs. Status updates in real time, no more "still working" spinner of doom.
  • HubSpot migration tool: pulls custom property definitions and applies them automatically.
  • Mailchimp import preserves your audience segments as Clientlink tags.
  • Audit log search by IP, user, or property name — finally.

Fixes

  • Fixed: edge case where Stripe webhook retries created duplicate "payment.succeeded" automation runs.
  • Fixed: dark-mode color contrast on email body previews in Safari 17.
  • Fixed: GET /v4/contacts sometimes returned 422 when cursor contained a + character.

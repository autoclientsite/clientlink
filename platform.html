Under the hood

The plumbing
nobody else builds.

The reason Clientlink replaces eight tools isn't features — it's that every module shares the same data model, identity layer, and audit log. So nothing falls through the cracks.

One identity layer

A contact is a contact — across CRM, campaigns, funnels, booking, and automations. No deduping, no merge tools, no spreadsheets at 2 AM.

SAML SSO & RBAC

Single sign-on, role-based access control, and granular permissions per workspace. Audit log for every action your team takes.

Real-time everywhere

A reply in the inbox updates the pipeline. A meeting booked fires the welcome flow. No syncs, no race conditions, no stale data.

120+ integrations

Stripe, Shopify, QuickBooks, Slack, Google, Meta, WhatsApp — plus a typed REST API and webhooks for everything else.

SOC 2 · GDPR · HIPAA

SOC 2 Type II and GDPR on every plan. HIPAA-eligible workloads on Scale with a signed BAA. Data residency: US / EU / AU.

24/7 humans

Chat support replies in under 4 minutes — by an engineer, not a script. Dedicated CSM on Scale, 99.99% uptime SLA, signed.
